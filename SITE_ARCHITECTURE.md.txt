# Corgi Capital — Site Architecture

This document defines the structure of the Corgi Capital website.

The site intentionally contains very few pages.

The goal is clarity and restraint.

---

# Core Pages

The site should contain a small number of pages.

Primary pages:

Home
Investments
Philosophy
Contact

Each page should maintain the same visual language.

---

# Homepage

Purpose:

Create intrigue and communicate conviction.

Structure:

Hero statement

WE KNOW SOMETHING

YOU DON'T

Selective Capital

Supporting signals:

Brand
Coordinates
Email

The homepage should remain minimal.

No long content.

---

# Investments Page

Purpose:

Provide credibility without overexposure.

Structure:

Minimal list of portfolio companies.

Example format:

Company name
Sector
Year

Avoid heavy descriptions.

---

# Philosophy Page

Purpose:

Explain investment thinking.

Content should remain concise.

Example sections:

Selective Capital
Long-term thinking
Conviction

Avoid corporate language.

---

# Contact Page

Purpose:

Provide a direct communication channel.

Minimal format:

Email
Optional short statement

Example:

hello@corgi-capital.com

---

# Navigation

Navigation should remain minimal.

Example:

Home
Investments
Philosophy
Contact

Navigation should not dominate the interface.

---

# SEO Strategy

The brand relies more on reputation and referrals than SEO.

However basic metadata should exist.

Homepage title:

Corgi Capital

Description:

Selective Capital

---

# Performance Requirements

The site must remain extremely lightweight.

Target:

<100kb HTML
Minimal JavaScript
No heavy libraries

Avoid frameworks unless necessary.

---

# AI Development Guidelines

When AI tools generate new pages they must:

Follow VISUAL_IDENTITY.md
Follow DESIGN_SYSTEM.md
Maintain minimal layout
Avoid adding unnecessary components

AI agents should prefer:

large typography
sparse layouts
text-based content

over graphical interfaces.

---

# Future Expansion

If the firm grows additional pages may be added.

Examples:

Portfolio
Writing
Research

However the minimalist philosophy should remain unchanged.

The homepage should always remain the strongest visual statement.
